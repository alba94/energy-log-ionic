Introduction & User Units
  • The app gathers your anthropometric data (age, gender, height, weight) to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) using Mifflin - St. Jeor equation
  • The next step is to determine your average activity level over 24 hours
  • Your BMR times your activity level plus daily exercises return an estimate of your total daily energy expenditure (TDEE)
